# Privacy Policy for Contact Card

**Effective Date:** July 21, 2026

## 1. Overview

Contact Card is a Discord application that helps communities create and share member contact cards.

This Privacy Policy explains what information Contact Card collects, how that information is used, and the choices available to users.

By using Contact Card, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.

## 2. Information Users Choose to Provide

Contact Card stores information that users voluntarily submit through the bot.

Depending on the available profile fields, this may include:

- Display name or preferred name
- Phone number
- Email address
- Birthday
- Social media usernames or links
- Discord user ID
- Discord username
- Discord server ID
- Other optional profile information made available through the bot

Users should only submit information they are comfortable sharing with other members of their Discord server.

## 3. How Information Is Used

Information submitted to Contact Card is used only to provide the bot’s features, including:

- Creating and displaying contact cards
- Allowing members of the same Discord server to view shared contact information
- Browsing the server’s member directory
- Updating or deleting saved profiles
- Generating contact-directory exports, including vCard files, when requested

Contact Card does not use submitted information for advertising, marketing, or behavioral profiling.

## 4. Who Can View Submitted Information

Contact Card is designed as a shared directory.

Information submitted through the bot may be viewed by other members of the Discord server in which the profile was created.

Members of that server may also be able to download shared contact information through directory-export features.

Server owners and administrators are responsible for deciding whether Contact Card is appropriate for their community and for informing members how the bot will be used.

## 5. Data Storage

Contact Card stores profile information only as needed to operate the bot.

Reasonable measures are used to protect stored information. However, no online service or storage system can guarantee complete security.

## 6. Data Deletion

Users may delete their saved profile information at any time by using the `/deleteprofile` command.

After deletion, the profile will no longer appear in the bot’s directory.

A user may also contact the developer regarding a data-deletion request.

## 7. Data Sharing and Sale

Contact Card does not sell personal information.

Contact Card does not share submitted profile information with advertisers or data brokers.

Information may be disclosed when required by law, necessary to protect the service or its users, or needed to investigate abuse or security concerns.

## 8. Third-Party Services

Contact Card operates through Discord and may rely on hosting, database, or infrastructure providers to function.

Use of Discord is also governed by Discord’s own terms and privacy practices.

External websites and social media links submitted by users are governed by the privacy policies of those third-party services.

## 9. Children’s Privacy

Contact Card is not intended for anyone who is not old enough to use Discord under Discord’s Terms of Service and applicable law.

The developer does not knowingly collect information from children who are prohibited from using Discord.

## 10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.

When changes are made, the Effective Date at the top of this page will be updated.

Continued use of Contact Card after an updated policy becomes effective constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
